About

The Creator:

I am Erica Holt, a Ph.D. Candidate in the History Department at Michigan State University. My dissertation research explores the medical authority of reproductive care providers in China and Taiwan. You can learn more about it through my site and contact here.




Technical information:

As a Digital Historian, I am both grateful for and committed to open-source materials.
The framework of this website was created in Bootstrap Studio.
The map for this project was created through Leaflet and the tiles were customized with MapboxStudio.

I learned so much in the process of making this website, but perhaps most importantly, I have learned that if you have a question, there is an answer for it on the internet. I am deeply indebted to all of the professionals who patiently explain to people like me how to make a button in CSS.
