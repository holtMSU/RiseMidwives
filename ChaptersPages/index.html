Chapter 1

Midiwife Training Programs in the PRC

In the early years of the PRC, the Ministry of Health tried to develop stronger networks of medical care. Midwife Training Programs were an integral part of this program that contribute to an improvement in infant and maternal health.

Chapter 2

Midwife Training Programs in Taiwan under ROC rule

Althought infant mortality rates decreased significantly during the Japanese colonial period in Taiwan, Taiwanese people still believed they needed greater access to midwife care. As a result, we can observe how people asked the government for the expansion of midwife training programs through government documents.
